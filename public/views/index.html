What is Phising?

Phishing attacks pose a significant threat to your online security. Stay vigilant by being cautious of unsolicited emails, messages, and calls requesting sensitive information. Always verify the legitimacy of websites and communications by checking for HTTPS, examining URLs, and contacting organizations directly through trusted channels. Keep your software and security systems up to date to protect against vulnerabilities. Educate yourself, your employees, friends, and family about the risks of phishing and how to recognize and report suspicious messages. By staying informed and practicing good security habits, you can reduce the risk of falling victim to phishing attacks and protect your personal and financial information.
